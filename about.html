Our Mission

The Light-Ion Physics Group is dedicated to advancing the understanding of light-ion interactions and their applications in nuclear and particle physics. We foster collaboration and knowledge sharing among researchers and students.

Research Areas

  • Deuteron breakup simulations
  • Transverse Momentum Distributions (TMDs)
  • SIDIS kinematics
  • Light-ion scattering experiments
  • Theoretical modeling and computational physics

Our Team

Our group consists of faculty, postdocs, graduate students, and collaborators from various institutions. We welcome new members interested in light-ion physics.
