About Us

This site is for organizing meetings and sharing resources for light-ion physics. Zoom meetings will happen once every two months. A goal of ours is to further collaboration between those who do low energy nuclear structure and those who study high-energy reactions, with a specific focus on the physics of the future Electron-Ion Collider (EIC)
